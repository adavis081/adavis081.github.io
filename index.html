About Me

Experienced data scientist with 3+ years of experience leveraging data analytics, machine learning, natural language processing and more in domains such as cybersecurity and consulting. Focused on improving processes within my team, mentoring peers on best practices, and fostering an environment of growth and excellence.

I am currently a Senior Data Scientist at Guidehouse providing Attack Surface Management (ASM) support to enhance cybersecurity of the Department of State (DOS). In this position, I am part of a team that ingests various data sources across the department, develops KPIs to track the health and readiness of the departments cyber readiness, and maintains several dashboards that allow leadership to quickly access and understand cybersecurity priorities.

Previously, I was a data scientist supporting the Department of Defense (DoD), working at the intersection of technology and operations. I supported USD(R&E) by helping the Department understand the impact of emerging technologies, develop S&T priorities and strategies, and understand its current portfolio.

I am also a Syracuse University graduate. I received an M.S. in Business Analytics, a B.S. in Economics, and a minor in Mathematics.
